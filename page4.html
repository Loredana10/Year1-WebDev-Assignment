    Travel Tips!

    1. Buy a small backpack or suitcase/ travel light.
    2. Take pictures of your luggage and clothes in case they get lost or stolen.
    3. Don't be afraid to use a map or ask for directions.
    4. Make extra copies of your passport and important documents.
    5. Learn basic phrases in the native language of your destination.
    6. Book flights 2-3 months in advance to get the best price.
